Introduction

BuzzMate is a social travel and chatting app that helps users connect with people travelling on the same train or similar route. This Privacy Policy applies to all users who create an account, browse profiles, chat, follow users, upload posts, interact with content, use notifications or use travel-related features inside BuzzMate.

By using BuzzMate, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

App Permissions We Use

BuzzMate requests permissions only to support app features. Permission access depends on your device settings and your choices.

✓Location permission is used for nearby users, route discovery and travel-related matching features.
✓Camera permission is used to capture photos or videos for profile, posts or chat media.
✓Photos and media permission is used when you select images or media from your device.
✓Microphone permission is used for voice messages or audio recording features.
✓Notification permission is used to send alerts for chats, follow requests, likes, comments, replies, posts and important app updates.
✓Internet permission is used to connect BuzzMate with online services and deliver app features.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, maintain, improve and protect BuzzMate.

✓Create and manage your BuzzMate account.
✓Show your profile and content according to app features.
✓Help users discover matching travellers, nearby profiles or route-based connections.
✓Enable chat, media sharing, follow requests, posts, likes, comments, reports and notifications.

05

Location and Travel Data

BuzzMate may use location and journey details to provide features such as nearby users, same train discovery, route matching and travel-related connections.

You should only add correct journey details. Do not share sensitive personal information such as home address, OTP, passwords, bank details, private documents or private photos with unknown users.

06

Chats, Posts and Public Content

Content you share through BuzzMate may be visible depending on the feature. For example, your profile, posts, comments, likes, follow activity and public interactions may be visible to other users according to app design.

Chat messages and media are used to provide messaging features between users. You are responsible for the messages and content you send. Do not send abusive, illegal, harmful, private or unsafe content.

07

Reports, Blocking and Safety

If you report a user, block a user or submit a safety complaint, BuzzMate may store related information to review the issue and take action.

✓Reports may include user IDs, reason, message details, screenshots or related content.
✓Blocked user data may be stored to prevent unwanted interaction.
✓Accounts violating safety rules may be restricted, suspended or permanently banned.
✓Repeated valid reports may lead to permanent account action based on severity.
08

Notifications

BuzzMate may use notification tokens to send push notifications for chats, follow requests, likes, comments, replies, posts, safety alerts and important app updates.

Routine app notifications may be automatically and permanently deleted after 30 days.

You can control notification permission from your device settings.

09

Service Providers and Backend Services

BuzzMate uses trusted cloud, backend, database, storage, authentication, notification, diagnostics and performance service providers to operate app features securely.

We do not publicly disclose backend configuration, project IDs, database URLs, storage bucket paths, security rules, API keys or internal server details in this Privacy Policy.

These service providers may process limited data only as needed to provide app functionality, security, storage, notifications and performance monitoring.

Third-Party Links and Railway Services

BuzzMate may provide access or navigation to railway-related websites or third-party services such as booking, PNR status, train status, train location or similar travel services.

BuzzMate does not control third-party websites, bookings, payments, cancellations, refunds, railway schedules, railway services or external platform privacy practices. When you open a third-party service, their own terms and privacy policy apply.

11

Data Sharing

BuzzMate does not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only when required to provide app features, protect users, comply with law or operate trusted technical services.

✓With other users, according to profile, chat, post, follow and travel discovery features.
✓With service providers who help run app features securely.
✓When required by law, legal process, safety investigation or government request.
✓To prevent fraud, abuse, harassment, spam, security issues or illegal activity.

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no online platform, app or internet-based service can guarantee 100% security.

Users should keep their login details safe, avoid sharing sensitive personal information and immediately report any suspicious activity or account misuse.

13

Data Retention

Routine app notifications may be automatically and permanently deleted after 30 days.

Posts, comments, replies or other user content may be hidden, marked as deleted, disabled or removed from public view when a user deletes them or when required for safety and moderation. Some deleted content may not be immediately or permanently erased from our systems.

BuzzMate may keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide app features, resolve disputes, enforce rules, prevent abuse or comply with legal requirements.

Some data such as reports, ban records, safety logs, abuse-related records, fraud prevention records, deleted-content records or legal records may be retained for longer to protect the platform and users.
